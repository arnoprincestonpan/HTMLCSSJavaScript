About Me

Who is behind all these articles? Me, Arno Princeston Pan. Yes, it's spelled Prin"ces"ton and not Prineston. I am a local Vancouverite located in the good old Lower Mainland of British Columbia. I spent the first 6 years in Taiwan. And immigrated to Canada. I have been here for over 2 decades and don't think I'll be leaving Canada any time soon. I briefly attended SFU after highschool for a year until I transferred to BCIT's Architectural Building Technology program. I worked for 3 years as an estimator with a few companies before COVID-19 hit and I was laid off. I have been working on redeveloping myself for a new career. Hopefully in Software Development on the Web. (i.e. Front-End, Back-End). I hope I can continue blogging in my own platform of choice. And improve my content. Thank you all for checking the website out.
